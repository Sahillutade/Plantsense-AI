CERTIFICATION SUMMARY
This certifies that pressure vessel V-12, installed 2012-11-05 at Unit 1 - Storage,
has been inspected in accordance with statutory requirements and is found fit for
continued service under the following conditions:

- Maximum allowable working pressure (MAWP): 18 bar
- Hydrostatic test pressure: 27 bar (test conducted 2025-07-01, see WO-3050)
- Thickness survey: within acceptable corrosion allowance (last survey WO-2940,
  2025-04-10, no defects found)
- Next statutory inspection due: 2026-07-01

CONDITIONS OF CERTIFICATION
1. Relief valve V-22 set pressure must be verified at or below MAWP at each
   inspection cycle.
2. Any modification to vessel nozzles or supports requires re-certification.

This certificate is valid until 2026-07-01 or until next statutory inspection,
whichever is earlier, per PESO Static and Mobile Pressure Vessel Rules.
