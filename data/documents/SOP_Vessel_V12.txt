STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
Document No: SOP-VESSEL-V12-01
Title: Operation and Monitoring of Pressure Vessel V-12
Unit: Unit 1 - Storage
Revision: 4
Effective Date: 2022-06-15

1. PURPOSE
This procedure defines the operating envelope and monitoring requirements for
pressure vessel V-12 (L&T Heavy Engineering PV-3000), used for intermediate
process storage.

2. OPERATING PARAMETERS
- Maximum allowable working pressure (MAWP): 18 bar
- Normal operating pressure: 12 - 15 bar
- Relief valve V-22 set pressure: 18 bar (must not exceed MAWP)
- Maximum fill level: 85% of vessel capacity

3. MONITORING REQUIREMENTS
3.1 Pressure and level shall be logged continuously via DCS.
3.2 Relief valve V-22 set pressure shall be verified at every statutory inspection
    cycle and after any vessel modification.
3.3 External corrosion inspection (visual) required quarterly; internal thickness
    survey required per PESO statutory schedule.

4. STATUTORY COMPLIANCE
This vessel is subject to PESO Static and Mobile Pressure Vessel Rules. Statutory
hydrostatic testing and certification renewal is required prior to certificate
expiry (see Compliance Certificate PESO-CERT-V12-2025). Operating beyond certificate
expiry without renewal is a regulatory violation and grounds for immediate shutdown.

5. KNOWN RISK AREAS
Relief valve V-22 is the primary overpressure protection for this vessel. Any
deviation in V-22 set pressure verification must be treated as a high-priority
finding, since V-12 has no secondary overpressure protection device.
