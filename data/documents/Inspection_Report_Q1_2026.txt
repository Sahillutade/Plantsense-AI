FINDINGS - P-204 (Centrifugal Pump)
Page 4
On 2026-03-04, condition monitoring logs showed vibration readings of 5.1 mm/s RMS,
exceeding the SOP-PUMP-204-01 threshold of 4.5 mm/s RMS. This reading was recorded
in the shift log but was not escalated for inspection per procedure. Vibration
continued to trend upward over the following five days. On 2026-03-09, pump P-204
experienced a mechanical seal rupture (see Work Order WO-3381), resulting in 14 hours
of unplanned downtime.

Root cause assessment: Failure to escalate the 2026-03-04 vibration exceedance per
SOP-PUMP-204-01 Section 3.2 was a contributing factor. This is the second seal-related
failure on P-204 in the past 12 months (also see WO-3275, November 2025).

Recommendation: Review condition monitoring alarm escalation workflow. Consider
automated escalation trigger rather than relying on manual shift log review.
