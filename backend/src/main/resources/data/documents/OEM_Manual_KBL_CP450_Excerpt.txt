OEM MAINTENANCE MANUAL EXCERPT
Manufacturer: Kirloskar Brothers
Model: KBL-CP-450 Centrifugal Pump Series
Section 7: Mechanical Seal Maintenance

7.1 SEAL TYPE
KBL-CP-450 series pumps are fitted with a single mechanical seal, flush-cooled,
requiring continuous flush flow of minimum 2 LPM to prevent dry-running damage to
seal faces.

7.2 COMMON FAILURE MODES
- Dry running due to loss of flush flow: causes rapid seal face wear, typically
  within hours of flow loss.
- Gradual seal face wear under normal operation: typically preceded by a rising
  vibration trend over several days as seal clearances increase.
- Thermal shock from process temperature excursions.

7.3 RECOMMENDED MONITORING
Kirloskar Brothers recommends continuous vibration monitoring (per ISO 10816-3)
as the primary early-warning indicator for seal degradation, since vibration trends
typically precede visible leakage or seal rupture by 3-7 days. Flush flow monitoring
alone is not sufficient, as it only detects failure after dry-running has begun.

7.4 RECOMMENDED SPARE PARTS INVENTORY
Maintain minimum 1 complete seal kit per installed unit for pumps in high-criticality
service.
