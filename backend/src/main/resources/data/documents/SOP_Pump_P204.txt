STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
Document No: SOP-PUMP-204-01
Title: Operation and Monitoring of Centrifugal Pump P-204
Unit: Unit 2 - Process Area
Revision: 3
Effective Date: 2024-02-10

1. PURPOSE
This procedure defines the operating envelope, monitoring requirements, and shutdown
criteria for centrifugal pump P-204 (Kirloskar Brothers KBL-CP-450).

2. OPERATING PARAMETERS
- Normal discharge pressure: 6.5 - 7.2 bar
- Maximum continuous vibration (ISO 10816 Zone B): 4.5 mm/s RMS
- Seal flush flow: minimum 2 LPM continuous

3. MONITORING REQUIREMENTS
3.1 Vibration readings shall be logged every shift via the condition monitoring system.
3.2 Any reading exceeding 4.5 mm/s RMS for more than 2 consecutive readings shall
    trigger a mandatory inspection within 24 hours, regardless of production schedule.
3.3 Operators shall not rely solely on automated alarms; manual log review is required
    at shift handover.

4. SHUTDOWN CRITERIA
Immediate shutdown is required if:
- Vibration exceeds 7.0 mm/s RMS
- Seal flush flow drops below 1 LPM
- Audible grinding or unusual noise is reported

5. KNOWN FAILURE MODES
Mechanical seal degradation is the most common failure mode for this pump class,
typically preceded by a gradual rise in vibration over 3-7 days before seal rupture.
Operators and maintenance teams should treat sustained vibration trends as an early
warning indicator rather than waiting for alarm thresholds to be breached.

6. REFERENCES
- OEM Manual: Kirloskar Brothers KBL-CP-450 Maintenance Guide
- ISO 10816-3 Mechanical Vibration Standards
- Inspection Report INS-Q1-2026
